## Summary
Improves screen reader accessibility for the `EmojiPicker` component by adding missing ARIA attributes.

## User impact
Screen reader users can now understand that the emoji button is a popup trigger, know whether it is expanded, and hear the currently selected emoji or the button's action. Inside the dropdown, the popover is properly announced as a dialog, and individual emoji buttons declare their contents correctly.

## What changed
- Added `aria-haspopup="dialog"`, `aria-expanded`, and a dynamic `aria-label` to the main toggle button in `EmojiPicker.tsx`.
- Added `role="dialog"` and `aria-label` to the dropdown container.
- Added `aria-label={emoji}` to the individual emoji options inside the picker grid.
- Added `dashboard/src/components/agent-manager/EmojiPicker.test.tsx` to assert these attributes.
