Replaces a raw `agentdesk.db.query("SELECT repo_id FROM kanban_cards ...")` call in `policies/pipeline.js`'s `onCardTransition` hook with the existing `agentdesk.cards.get` typed facade.

This directly reduces policy coupling to the underlying `kanban_cards` SQLite/PostgreSQL schema while maintaining perfect behavioral parity. A dedicated unit test has been added to prove that the typed facade is invoked correctly.
